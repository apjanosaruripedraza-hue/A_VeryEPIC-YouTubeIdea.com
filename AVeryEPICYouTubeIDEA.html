OUR CHANNEL WILL BE KNOWN FOR

Our Youtube Channel will be known for it's home cooking. We create videos that show how to prepare simple, delicious meals in a way that feels approachable and fun. The goal is to inspire viewers to cook at home, learn new techniques, and enjoy the process of making food. It will have a original appraoch of using GACHA LIFE characters to represent the chefs, recorders, and VA's to make it extra fun and original.
Our Videos will fall under entertainment and education, we'll entertain our viewers by having short stories, fun games, and a bit of trend settings. While doing so, we'll also talk about Sitio Bebe in a way that isn't too over bearing or too much to the fact that it's not even a cooking show anymore. It will have it's own section, of course!

WHO IS OUR TARGET AUDIENCE

Our target audience is mainly ages 12–19, especially beginners like teenagers, college students, or young people cooking while their parents are at work. However, the content is also suitable for older viewers who want to learn basic cooking or start cooking as a hobby.

WHAT WE NEED TO BE A CHANNEL

To start our cooking channel, we need a few essentials to make everything run smoothly. A budget is important because we’ll need money for ingredients like rice, meat, vegetables, and fruits. The right materials such as cookware, utensils, and storage containers are necessary for preparing, cooking, and keeping food fresh.

Kitchen essentials like a stove, cleaning tools, and safety items helps us cook safely and keep our space clean.

Finally, basic skills in cooking methods, portioning, and ingredient substitutions are needed so we can prepare meals confidently and adjust when something is missing. These basics make sure our cooking channel is practical, safe, and enjoyable to watch, including a few

Cooking Tips!

-Always be attentive to what you are doing
-Always prepare materials and ingredients
-Pay full attention to both the cooking book you're using and the food you are making
-Be safe
-Use quality ingredients
-Don't fear failure
-Enjoy your time cooking

WHY WAS THIS CHANNEL CREATED

This channel was created to provide teenagers with knowledge about a dish that is easy to prepare and can be made by anyone. This aims to offer entertainment, learning, and encourage independent skills. But most importantly, we created this channel to raise funds for Sitio Bebe because we believe that through this, we can help the community and gather support from different people to make a positive impact.

Our goal is not only to share easy and delicious recipes but also to inspire kindness, unity, and compassion among everyone who supports our cause. With your kindness and support for our channel, we find hope for a better future for Sitio Bebe.
